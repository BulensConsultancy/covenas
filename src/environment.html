Project AWARE

With all the certifications issued, we make a donation to Project AWARE, an independent, non profit organization with the aim to protect and care about the marine enviroment (www.projectaware.org). Besides, during the courses and in the dives, we put a special emphasis on responsible behaviur, following the 10 tips for responsible divers and organizing beach cleanups. Compromised with being an autosustainable company, we always try to reduce debris, one way to achieve this is by giving our students digital books rather than paper.

In what comes next are 10 tips for divers to protected to ocean planet.
